# Privacy Policy for Bright Head
Last updated: March 5, 2026

Bright Head (“we”, “our”, “the app”) provides wellness support and trigger-regulation guidance.  
Bright Head is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

## 1. Data We Access

### Health Data (Apple HealthKit, Apple Watch)
If you grant permission, the app may access:
- Heart rate
- Heart rate variability (HRV)
- Workout session context used to enable live heart-rate collection

### Motion/Activity Data
If you grant permission, the app may access motion and step-related data to detect activity context (for example, walking/exercise gating logic).

### User-Provided Content
If you use Relationship Reminders, the app stores:
- Text captions you enter
- Photos you select

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data to:
- Estimate wellness trigger state in real time
- Show calming tools and guidance
- Display live status between paired Apple Watch and iPhone

We do not use your data for advertising.

## 3. Storage and Sharing

- Relationship reminder text and photos are stored locally on your device.
- Live status signals are exchanged between your paired watch and iPhone using Apple WatchConnectivity.
- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not share HealthKit data with third parties for marketing.

## 4. Health and Safety Notice

Bright Head is intended for general wellness support only.  
It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition.  
If you feel unwell or have concerning symptoms, contact a qualified healthcare professional.

## 5. Permissions

You may deny or revoke permissions at any time in iOS/watchOS Settings:
- Health permissions (Health app / Settings)
- Motion & Fitness
- Notifications
- Photos access (if using reminder photos)

Some features may not work without the required permissions.

## 6. Children

Bright Head is not directed to children under 13.  
If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us to request deletion.

## 7. Data Retention

User-entered reminder content remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or uninstall the app.

## 8. International Users

If you use the app outside your home country, your data is still processed primarily on-device.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
The “Last updated” date will indicate the latest version.
